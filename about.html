About MachSafe

Building safety technology
for a safer UK.

MachSafe is a UK-based startup on a mission to make Building Safety Act 2022 compliance accessible, affordable, and continuous for every Accountable Person managing a higher-risk residential building in England.

Mission & Vision

Why MachSafe exists.

72 people died in the Grenfell Tower fire in 2017 because building safety information was not properly managed. MachSafe was created to ensure this never happens again.

Our Mission
To make Building Safety Act 2022 compliance accessible, affordable, and continuous for every Accountable Person managing a higher-risk residential building in England — protecting residents' lives through better compliance tools.
Our Vision
To become the default compliance infrastructure for building safety regulation in the United Kingdom and internationally — the platform every Accountable Person relies on to demonstrate their building is safe.
Our Values

What we believe in.

Safety First
Every feature we build is in service of one goal — ensuring that people living in high-rise residential buildings are protected by properly managed safety records.
Honest AI
Our AI is a tool for human experts, not a replacement for them. Every AI output is clearly labelled as a draft requiring human review and approval before use.
Trust Through Transparency
We maintain complete transparency about how MachSafe works, what data we hold, and how it is protected. Compliance professionals deserve a platform they can trust completely.
