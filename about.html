About MachSafe

Building safety technology
for a safer UK.

MachSafe is a UK-based startup on a mission to make Building Safety Act 2022 compliance accessible, affordable, and continuous for every Accountable Person managing a higher-risk residential building in England.

Mission & Vision

Why MachSafe exists.

72 people died in the Grenfell Tower fire in 2017 because building safety information was not properly managed. MachSafe was created to ensure this never happens again.

🎯
Our Mission
To make Building Safety Act 2022 compliance accessible, affordable, and continuous for every Accountable Person managing a higher-risk residential building in England — protecting residents' lives through better compliance tools.
🔭
Our Vision
To become the default compliance infrastructure for building safety regulation in the United Kingdom and internationally — the platform every Accountable Person relies on to demonstrate their building is safe.
The Founder

Built by someone who knows
the regulation inside out.

VS
Venkatesh Sethu
Founder & CEO, MachSafe

Venkatesh identified the MachSafe opportunity during his MSc in Construction Engineering Management at the University of East London in 2023–2024, where he studied the Building Safety Act 2022 directly during the period it came into force. His research revealed that 12,500 occupied higher-risk residential buildings in England now face legally mandated compliance obligations with no adequate digital solution available.

Before founding MachSafe, Venkatesh gained practical engineering and compliance experience across multiple regulated environments — from a ₹1,520 million regulated infrastructure project in India to managing health and safety compliance at a regulated petroleum retail site in London.
