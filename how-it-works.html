MachSafe is designed for building safety managers, not software engineers. Five steps from setup to your first Safety Case Report.

1

Add your building

Enter your building details — address, height, number of floors, number of units, and construction type. MachSafe immediately maps your legal compliance obligations under the Building Safety Act 2022.

  • Takes less than 5 minutes to set up
  • No technical knowledge required
  • Supports multiple buildings simultaneously
  • Automatically identifies your specific obligations based on building parameters

3

System analyses compliance

MachSafe's compliance engine analyses every document against the Building Safety Act 2022 requirements. It identifies missing documents, inadequate content, and expiring documents — all ranked by legal risk severity.

  • Checks all 14 mandatory document categories
  • Risk-weighted scoring using building-specific parameters
  • Explains which regulation each gap violates
  • Updates in real time as you upload new documents

4

Fix gaps

Follow the prioritised action list to resolve compliance gaps. Upload missing documents, update expired ones, and track your progress in real time. Your compliance score updates instantly as you work through each item.

  • Prioritised action list — fix highest risk first
  • Upload directly from the action list
  • Assign tasks to team members
  • Progress tracked with completion percentages

5

Generate your Safety Case Report

Once your documents are in order, MachSafe's AI generates a compliant Safety Case Report draft using your building's own data. Review each section, make any edits, approve, and download — in hours, not weeks.

  • AI draft generated from your building's specific data
  • Structured to BSR Safety Case guidance format
  • Section-by-section review and approval workflow
  • Full audit trail of AI output and human approvals
  • Export as PDF for BSR submission
