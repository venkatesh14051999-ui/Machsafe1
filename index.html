The Problem

Building safety compliance
is complex.

The Building Safety Act 2022 created legally mandated obligations for thousands of organisations. Most are struggling with the same four problems.

Documents are scattered
Safety documents are stored across email, SharePoint, and paper files — with no version control, no structure, and no audit trail.
Hard to track missing items
There is no easy way to know which documents are missing, which are expiring, or which gaps create the most serious legal risk.
Risk of non-compliance
Non-compliance carries personal criminal liability and unlimited financial penalties for Accountable Persons and directors.
Time-consuming reports
Safety Case Reports require qualified engineers charging up to £8,000 per building. Most organisations cannot afford this at scale.
